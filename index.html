Meet your teacher —

Rosebel Jane Bacoy

I'm a CELTA-certified English teacher who has spent the last seven years working almost entirely online with students preparing for exams, interviews, and simply the next conversation they're nervous about.

Most of my students don't need more grammar rules — they need a place to try sentences out loud before the meeting, the interview, or the test actually happens. That's what our lessons are for.

"I believe language learning should be active, relevant, and immersive — I focus on communicative competence: the skills and confidence to actually use English in the real world, not just describe it correctly on paper."
